## FAQ

### Can I change plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my limits?
For message limits, your service will be rate-limited until the next billing cycle or you can upgrade. For storage limits, you'll need to delete content or upgrade.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, all paid tiers include a free trial period. No credit card required for Free tier.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. No long-term contracts required (except custom Enterprise agreements).

### What is your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans. Monthly plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled anytime.
